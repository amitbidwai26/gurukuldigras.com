Our Mission Statement By 2022

By 2022, school aims to plant 2000 plants, each plant on the Birthday of the student studying in the school.School promises to nurture, nourish and develop these plants in to beautiful green trees spreading the greenery and shadow in school premises.All these plants would be botanically important species which will help the students to study flora. School also aims to create spoken English culture among students and achieve hundred percent success in it by 2022. The school also targets to increase its strength more than 1000 students admitting maximum students from local town
