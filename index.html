About School

Gurukul English Medium School, Digras is a Co-Educational school affiliated to CBSE syllabus. Gurukul English Medium School, Digras is located in Yavatmal, Maharashtra. The school was established in the year 2007 and is managed by Shree Balaji Bahuuddeshiya Shikshan Sanstha.

Facilities provided by Gurukul English Medium School, Digras

Physics Lab, Chemistry Lab, Biology Lab, Maths Lab, Computer Science Lab, Library, Indoor Games, Dance Rooms, Music Rooms, Health and Medical Check up

Gurukul English Medium School, Digras has Library facility with over 1500 books.
